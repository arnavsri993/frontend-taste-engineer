# Tailwind blocks and templates

Seed families include Tailwind Plus/UI, Preline, Flowbite, daisyUI, Mamba UI, Meraki UI, Tailblocks, Float UI, Cruip, TailAwesome, Tailwind Toolbox, Kometa, Creative Tim, Tailwind Kit, Tailwind Components, Material Tailwind, Pagedone, Shuffle, TailTemplate, Notus, Tailwind Awesome, and shadcn-compatible block registries.

## Use by stage

- Brief: consult only section-pattern summaries to explore information architecture. Do not choose a template before the product thesis.
- Planning: compare navigation, hero, feature, proof, pricing, FAQ, footer, dashboard, settings, and form structures against actual content and responsive constraints.
- Implementation: use a block only after license, entitlement, dependency, semantics, state, and theming review.
- Refinement: change the block’s hierarchy, rhythm, typography, material, media, and component styling until it belongs to the product.
- Verification: check originality, notices, responsive interpolation, keyboard/focus, content expansion, dark/high-contrast modes when promised, and production output.

## Sequencing and density

Sequence sections by user decision: identify the product and audience, establish the job/outcome, explain mechanism, provide verifiable proof, resolve objections, show terms/pricing when real, and offer a truthful next action. Do not force every page through a hero–three cards–testimonials–pricing–FAQ sequence.

Match density to use: marketing sections can breathe when content is sparse; dashboards and settings need compact, scannable alignment. Avoid wrapping every item in a rounded card or using responsive stacking as the only layout strategy.

## Responsive and theming cautions

- Preserve semantic source order when grids rearrange.
- Test intermediate widths, long labels, zoom/reflow, short viewports, and localization expansion.
- Map copied utility values to project tokens instead of preserving arbitrary one-off colors, radii, shadows, and spacing.
- Verify light/dark/high-contrast parity only for supported modes; do not inherit a theme toggle the product cannot maintain.
- Remove unused scripts, icon packs, plugins, fonts, and demo data.

## License gate

“Free,” “copy/paste,” visible HTML, or client-rendered source does not establish reuse rights. Confirm whether the exact block is open, paid, account-bound, attribution-required, or governed by separate asset/font/icon terms. Premium/pro material is link-only until entitlement and terms are verified for the current project.
